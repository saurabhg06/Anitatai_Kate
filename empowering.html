Our Key Servises

Under the visionary leadership of Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate, our initiatives focus on empowering communities through impactful programs, transparent governance, and citizen-driven development. From social welfare and health drives to education, environmental care, and women empowerment, every service reflects a deep commitment to public good and sustainable progress.

Wall of Humanity

To support the needy and promote kindness, a social initiative called the Wall of Humanity was started at PK Chowk, Pimple Saudagar, based on the noble concept of Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate.The initiative encourages people to donate essential items and help those in need, spreading compassion within the community.

Saudagar Premier League

The Saudagar Cricket Premier League was organized by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate in Pimple Saudagar to promote the spirit of sportsmanship among the youth. The tournament featured exciting matches and a range of attractive prizes, encouraging young players to stay active, competitive, and united through the love of cricket.

Robotic Day Celebration

An innovative initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate in collaboration with Challenger Public School was organized in Pimple Saudagar. The event aimed to encourage children to explore creativity, technology, and innovation through hands-on robotics activities. It was an inspiring day that sparked curiosity and future-ready thinking among young minds!

Jallosh Shopping Festival

The Jallosh Shopping Festival was organized by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate in Pimple Saudagar, bringing joy and excitement to the community. For the first time after the Corona pandemic, people experienced a heart-warming shopping celebration filled with energy and togetherness. The event featured Zumba sessions.

Women’s Day Celebration

An empowering initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate was organized in Pimple Saudagar to celebrate the strength and achievements of women. The event aimed to inspire confidence, unity, and empowerment among women from all walks of life. Together, let’s continue to support and uplift every woman in our community!

Happy Street Initiative

Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate has always emphasized that the residents of Pimple Saudagar should live happily and healthily. To spread joy and positivity, the Happy Street initiative was organized, featuring activities like yoga, Zumba, cycling, sports, painting competitions, selfie points, children’s play areas.

Cloth Bag Distribution Drive

An inspiring initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate was organized in Pimple Saudagar to promote the use of eco-friendly cloth bags. The drive aimed to encourage citizens to avoid plastic and protect our environment from pollution.

Blood Donation Camp

A noble initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate was organized in Pimple Saudagar on the occasion of Mr. Sandeep Vitthal Kate’s birthday. Many citizens came forward to donate blood, spreading the message of humanity and compassion. A heartfelt thank you to all the donors for making this initiative a great success!

World Environment Day

An inspiring initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate was organized in Pimple Saudagar to spread awareness about environmental conservation. Citizens actively participated in tree plantation and cleanliness activities, promoting a greener and healthier community.
