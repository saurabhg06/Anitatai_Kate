Work & Devlopment Insight

Formerly known as an agrarian zone a little or more than a decade ago, Pimple Saudagar is now a happening neighbourhood where IT professionals, famous personalities, government officers, and hoteliers live. It was Shatrughna Kate who brought good fortune to the area's residents after implementing new development plans.

Blood Donation Camp

A noble initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate was organized in Pimple Saudagar on the occasion of Mr. Sandeep Vitthal Kate’s birthday. Many citizens came forward to donate blood, spreading the message of humanity and compassion. A heartfelt thank you to all the donors for making this initiative a great success!

Women’s Day Celebration

An empowering initiative by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate was organized in Pimple Saudagar to celebrate the strength and achievements of women. The event aimed to inspire confidence, unity, and empowerment among women from all walks of life. Together, let’s continue to support and uplift every woman in our community!

World Environment Days

Once an agrarian region, Pimple Saudagar has transformed into a vibrant community under the visionary efforts of Shatrughna Kate. Guided by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate’s commitment to environmental care, initiatives like tree plantation and cleanliness drives continue to foster a greener, healthier, and more progressive neighbourhood!

Jallosh Shopping Festival

The Jallosh Shopping Festival was organized by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate in Pimple Saudagar, bringing joy and excitement to the community. For the first time after the Corona pandemic, people experienced a heart-warming shopping celebration filled with energy and togetherness. The event featured Zumba sessions, a children’s play area, aerobics, delicious food, and live band performances — creating a truly festive atmosphere for everyone.

Wall of Humanity/h5>

To support the needy and promote kindness, a social initiative called the Wall of Humanity was started at PK Chowk, Pimple Saudagar, based on the noble concept of Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate. The initiative encourages people to donate essential items and help those in need, spreading compassion within the community. social unity by spreading the spirit of care and humanity across Pimple Saudagar.

Saudagar Premier League

The Saudagar Cricket Premier League was organized by Mrs. Anitatai Sandeep Kate in Pimple Saudagar to promote the spirit of sportsmanship among the youth. The tournament featured exciting matches and a range of attractive prizes, encouraging young players to stay active, competitive, and united through the love of cricket.
